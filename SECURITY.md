# Security and privacy notice
 
Please do not submit issues, pull requests, discussions, screenshots, videos, or files containing personal health information, identifiable participant data, patient videos, private server details, credentials, tokens, certificates, or other sensitive information.
 
## Reporting privacy or security concerns
 
If you identify a potential privacy, security, or sensitive-information concern in this repository, please contact the corresponding authors privately rather than posting the information in a public issue.
 
When reporting a concern, please include a concise description of the affected file or page and why it may be sensitive. Do not include patient data, credentials, or other sensitive material in the report itself unless a secure institutional channel has been arranged.
 
## Supported versions
 
The public materials in this repository are manuscript-associated research materials and are not production software releases. No clinical or production deployment version is supported by this repository.
 
## Clinical-use boundary
 
The materials are not intended for clinical deployment, diagnostic use, treatment recommendation, regulatory submission, or replacement of standard uroflowmetry or clinician interpretation.
